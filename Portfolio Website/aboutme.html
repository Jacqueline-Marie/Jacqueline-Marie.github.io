About Me

My name is Jacqueline Doucette and I am a senior at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am graduating with both English and BDIC in Fashion Design and Merchandising majors, as well as a Certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. I have experience with MadCap Flare, Adobe InDesign and Photoshop, and HTML and CSS coding.

I love to paint and draw. My favorite mediums to work with are charcoal and watercolors. I also love to write, and mainly focus on my poetry.
